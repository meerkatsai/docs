Builtwith
---------
This integration leverages BuiltWith's technology detection capabilities to identify the software stack used by a specific website. By analysing the site's source code, BuiltWith provides detailed information about the technologies, frameworks, and tools implemented on the target domain. This action enables meerkats AI users to seamlessly incorporate BuiltWith's comprehensive technology profiling into their workflows, enhancing their ability to gather and utilise website intelligence.
