meerkats AI offers a suite of AI-powered tools that enable you to enrich, analyze, and extract data from various sources effortlessly. Each tool is designed for specific tasks, from web searching to scraping URLs, analyzing files, or enriching data with external APIs. Understanding how and when to use these tools will help you extract the most valuable insights and optimize your data analysis process.

#### [Available Tools](#available-tools)

1.  **Web Agent:** Ideal for browsing the web to retrieve public information such as official websites, news articles, or industry insights.
    
2.  **Scrape URL:** Extracts detailed information directly from a specified URL, allowing you to capture specific data points from web pages.
    
3.  **Instructions Only:** Synthesizes content based on existing inputs from your table, perfect for summarizing, analyzing, or generating text content without accessing external data sources.
    
4.  **Data Enrichment:** Uses external APIs to enrich your data with detailed company or personal information, such as contact details, employee counts, and more.
